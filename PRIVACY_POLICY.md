# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Scam Scanner ("we", "us", "our", "App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

**Your privacy is important to us.** We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our data practices.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

**Images and Documents:**
- Photos and documents you capture or upload for scanning
- This content is processed by AI to detect potential scams

**Location Information (Optional):**
- Precise or approximate location data when you choose to enable location features
- Used to map scam reports in your area
- You can disable location access at any time in your device settings

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifiers

**Usage Data:**
- Scan history (timestamps, analysis results)
- Features used within the App
- Crash reports and performance data

**Local Storage:**
- Scan results and history stored locally on your device
- App preferences and settings
- Usage statistics (monthly scan count, premium status)

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- We do NOT create user accounts or profiles
- We do NOT track your identity across other apps or websites
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 To Provide Services

- **AI Analysis:** Process images to detect scams and provide risk assessments
- **Scan History:** Store your scan results locally on your device
- **Learning Center:** Provide educational content about scams
- **Location Features:** Display scam reports on a map (when enabled)

### 3.2 To Improve the App

- **Performance Monitoring:** Identify and fix bugs and crashes
- **Service Enhancement:** Improve AI accuracy and user experience
- **Usage Analytics:** Understand which features are most valuable

### 3.3 To Manage Subscriptions

- **Payment Processing:** Handle premium subscriptions and token purchases through your app store
- **Usage Tracking:** Monitor scan limits for free and premium users

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored **locally on your device**:
- Scan history and results
- Images you've analyzed
- App preferences
- Usage statistics

This data is NOT transmitted to our servers and remains on your device.

### 4.2 External Processing

**AI Analysis:**
- Images are sent to AI service providers (OpenAI and/or Rork AI Toolkit) for analysis
- These services process images to generate scam detection results
- Images are not stored by us or our AI providers after processing
- See Section 7 for third-party service details

**Location Data:**
- If enabled, location coordinates may be stored locally with scan results
- Location data is NOT transmitted to external servers

### 4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Secure data transmission (HTTPS/TLS encryption)
- Local data storage with device-level encryption
- No persistent storage of images on external servers
- Regular security updates and patches

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Local Data
- Scan history is stored locally until you delete it
- History is limited to your 50 most recent scans
- You can clear your history at any time through the App

### 5.2 Processing Data
- Images sent for AI analysis are processed in real-time
- Images are NOT retained by AI providers after analysis is complete
- Temporary processing data is automatically deleted

## 6. Your Privacy Rights and Controls

### 6.1 Access and Control

You have full control over your data:
- **View History:** Access all your scan results in the App
- **Delete Scans:** Remove individual scans from history
- **Clear All Data:** Delete entire scan history
- **Manage Permissions:** Control camera, photo library, and location access through device settings

### 6.2 Location Privacy

- Location features are **optional** and disabled by default
- You can enable/disable location access at any time
- Location data is only collected when you explicitly grant permission
- Go to Settings → Privacy → Location Services to manage access

### 6.3 Camera and Photos

- Camera and photo library access is required for scanning functionality
- You grant permission each time you use these features
- No photos are accessed without your explicit action
- Manage permissions in your device settings

### 6.4 Data Portability

Your data is stored locally on your device. You can:
- Export scan results (screenshots or manual copying)
- Delete the App to remove all local data
- Transfer data by backing up your device

### 6.5 Right to Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:
- Clear scan history within the App
- Uninstall the App to remove all local data
- No residual data remains on our servers

## 7. Third-Party Services

### 7.1 AI Processing

**OpenAI API / Rork AI Toolkit:**
- Used to analyze images and detect scams
- Images are transmitted securely for real-time processing
- No images are stored after processing
- Subject to provider's privacy policy

### 7.2 Payment Processing

**Apple App Store / Google Play Store:**
- Handles all payments for subscriptions and token purchases
- We do not collect or store payment information
- Subject to your app store's privacy policy

### 7.3 Analytics (If Applicable)

We may use privacy-focused analytics services to understand app usage:
- Only aggregate, anonymized data is collected
- No personally identifiable information is included
- You can opt out through device settings

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## 10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:
- **Right to Know:** What personal information is collected
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt-out of data "sales" (we do not sell data)
- **Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate for exercising rights

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you have direct control over your information.

## 11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

European users have rights under GDPR:
- **Access:** Right to access your personal data
- **Rectification:** Right to correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure:** Right to delete your data
- **Portability:** Right to transfer your data
- **Object:** Right to object to data processing

Since data is stored locally, you can exercise these rights directly through the App or your device settings.
